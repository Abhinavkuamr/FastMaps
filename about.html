About Us

Who we are and what is our goal

Our Vision

FastMaps is a mobile app that helps users discover the best places to visit in their local area. The app is designed to be user-friendly and intuitive, making it easy for tourists to navigate and explore their surroundings. FastMaps works by using GPS technology to locate the user and provide recommendations for nearby attractions, restaurants, shops, and other points of interest..

Our Team

FastMaps was developed by a group of students who are passionate to solve real-world problems. Each member brings a unique skillset to the project, from software development to UX design. Our team is committed to innovation and excellence, and we are proud to have created an app that helps connect businesses with customers in a meaningful way.

Problem to Solve

While there are many map apps available, FastMaps is unique because it focuses specifically on helping users discover local businesses and attractions. The app works with local businesses to promote their offerings and provide users with a comprehensive guide to the area. This makes it easier for tourists to find the best places to eat, shop, and explore, without having to rely on generic recommendations or online reviews.

Future

FastMaps is currently available in select cities and countries, but we are constantly expanding our coverage. We are committed to providing users with the best possible experience, and we are always looking for new ways to improve and grow. If your city or country is not currently supported, we encourage you to check back in the future or contact us to request that we add it to our list.

Support Us

You can help us develop FastMaps further by sending donations to our Kickstarter. We do everything in our power to keep our app free for all to use, and we intend to keep it that way. Your donations make this all possible.
