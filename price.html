Support Us

by FastMaps pvt. ltd.

You can help our team

We believe that FastMaps has the potential to transform the way that tourists explore new cities and connect with local businesses. However, developing and maintaining an app like this requires a lot of time, effort, and resources. That's why we are asking for your support.

One way to support FastMaps is by promoting your business on the app. By purchasing a paid advertisement, our team will work closely with you to create an advertisement that is tailored to your business and its unique offerings. By advertising on FastMaps, you can help support our project while also promoting your business to potential customers.

We also accept Donataions

In addition to paid advertisements, we also accept donations. We believe that everyone should have access to FastMaps, regardless of their ability to pay. That's why we have set up a donation system to help fund the development and maintenance of the app. We accept traditional payment methods, as well as cryptocurrency donations, including Bitcoin and Ethereum.

Your donation will help us to continue improving and expanding the app, as well as providing better support for our users. Every donation, no matter how small, is greatly appreciated and will go directly towards making FastMaps the best app it can be.
